About the Band

Formed in in Dublin in 2011 with the aim of playing original Rock Music with a bluesy edge in the vein of Alice In Chains, Soundgarden, Gun's N'Roses, Captain Beefheart, Thin Lizzy, ACDC, Faith No More, The Doors etc etc We work hard at what we do and have achieved good acclaim with audiences and promoters alike in Dublin venues such as Sweeneys, Whelans, Grand Social and various house parties The band is currently in the studio working on their second 6 track EP.
